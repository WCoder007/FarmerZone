Current Openings


In the area of IoT, embedded systems and data analysis

Positions are available for motivated undergraduate and MS level graduate students. Most of this work is applied in nature with possibilities for internship and entrepreneurship. Students are expected to have one or more of : very strong programming skills, handyman skills and a passion for practical implementation of their designs.

In the area of nanoelectronics

Positions are available for both MS and PhD students. Students will be expected to be strong in mathematical analysis, have a good understanding of basic electronic device operation and modelling from their undergraduate coursework, hardworking, good communication skills.

If interested in any of the above positions, please send an e-mail to srikant@iitmandi.ac.in with your resume, a clearly written statement of specific interests in working with us, how are you a good fit for the above position and plans after completing your training.
